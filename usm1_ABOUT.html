USM has been a Swiss family run business since 1885.

The USM Haller along with our other ranges of tables and accessories all show our
commitment to unparalleled innovation, genuine quality and true style.

As well as creating world-class products, we care about building lasting relationships with
our customers. Our work to keep the environmental impact of our products to a minimum
has been rewarded with prestigious Greenguard certification for low chemical and
particle emissions.

Our Values

USM has been family-run for generations. As a result, our core values run
deep and permeate every element of what we do - from building furniture
to building relationships.

Modularity
